About FaculTrack

FaculTrack simplifies academic tracking and management for institutions, helping both students and educators enhance engagement and progress.

Our Mission

To streamline faculty performance tracking and evaluation through efficient, user-friendly technology for educational institutions.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform that empowers educational excellence by transforming faculty performance management.

How FaculTrack Works

Faculty Login & Dashboard

Faculty members log in securely and access their personalized dashboard to manage attendance, activities, and performance data.

Performance Tracking

FaculTrack collects and analyzes data like lectures delivered, projects guided, and research work to evaluate faculty performance.

Reporting & Feedback

Admins and stakeholders receive automated reports and actionable insights to recognize excellence and support faculty development.
